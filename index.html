--- layout: page title: Using R for Fisheries Analyses ---

\href{https://www.r-project.org/}{R is an environment} for statistical analyses and graphics that is rapidly gaining popularity with fisheries scientists. The fishR website provides resources for and examples of fisheries-related analyses performed in R. It is my hope that the resources here will inspire or motivate you to either use R or expand your knowledge of R for fisheries-related analyses. Take a look around!!

See \href{http://www.inside-r.org/what-is-r}{Inside-R} and \href{https://www.r-project.org/about.html}{R-project} for two descriptions of R.

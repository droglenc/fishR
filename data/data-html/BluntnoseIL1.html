BluntnoseIL1 {FSAdata}R Documentation

Subampled lengths of Bluntnose Minnows from Inch Lake, WI.

Description

Total lengths for a subsample from 144 Bluntnose Minnows (Pimephales notatus) from Inch Lake, WI in May, 2007.

Format

A data frame of 25 observations on the following 3 variables:

  • netID: a netID factor (all 41)
  • species: species name (all Bluntnose)

  • tl: total length (inches to nearest 0.1)

Topic(s)

  • Length Frequency
  • Length Expansion
  • Size Structure
  • PSD

Source

Derek H. Ogle, personal collection

See Also

InchLake1, InchLake2.
